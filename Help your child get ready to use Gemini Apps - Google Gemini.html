Gemini Access Update
Manage your child’s access to Gemini Apps.
Manage your child’s access to Gemini with Google’s Family Link
Hi FirstName,
Gemini Apps will soon be available for your child.
That means that your child will be able to use Gemini to find inspiration, learn new things, and do things like:
• Create stories, songs, and poetry
• Ask questions
• Get homework help and more
You’re in control with Family Link
Family Link is a parental controls app that helps you guide your child’s online experience. Family Link allows you to manage their important account and data settings.
Your child will be able to access Gemini Apps soon. We’ll also let you know when your child accesses Gemini for the first time.
You can manage these settings, including turning access off, from the Family Link app or familylink.google.com. Learn more.
Help your child get ready to use Gemini
Together, you and your child can get to know Gemini and learn how to use AI tools responsibly by checking out these resources:
• Get to know what Gemini Apps can do. Read FAQ
• Use this conversation guide to talk about AI with your family
• Learn the five must-knows to get started with AI. Watch video
Some helpful reminders
Gemini can make mistakes, so help your child think critically about Gemini responses.
• Remind your child that Gemini isn’t human. Even though it sometimes talks like one, it can’t think for itself or feel emotions
• Show your child how to double-check responses
• Let your child know not to enter sensitive or personal info in Gemini
• Our filters try to limit access to inappropriate content, but they’re not perfect.
Your child may encounter content you don’t want them to see
• Google Assistant Parental Controls will remain in Family Link and will continue to apply to Google Assistant devices in the home but will not apply to Gemini
How your child can access Gemini
When your child is signed in to a device or Gemini with their Google Account, here’s what they’ll be able to do:
• Use Gemini Apps on Android, iOS, and the web
• Set Gemini as their mobile assistant on their Android device
• Use Gemini on Android phones and tablets, even from the lock screen. Learn more
• Get help from Google Assistant for certain actions. When that happens, relevant Assistant settings, like Personal Results, apply. Learn more
As a reminder, Gemini Apps are new and evolving. They’re a part of our effort to develop LLMs responsibly, and Google is committed to working to improve over time. Learn more.
Google Terms apply to Gemini Apps. Google will process your child's data as described in the Google Privacy Policy and the Gemini Apps Privacy Notice.
